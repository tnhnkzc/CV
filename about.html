Who am I?

I was born on 1st of December in 1997 in Turkey.

I grew up in a city called "Mersin".
It's in the south of Turkey and the neighbour of the Mediterranean Sea.

My Education

When I was in high school, my dream was to study computer engineering, but, life conditions did not allow that to happen. I knew I was always good at English so I wanted to try myself on the English exam. And guess what? I got in to the university in my city to become an English teacher! After studying 4 years at Mersin University, it was time to gain some experience.

Gap Year

I had a gap year starting from September 2020 until September 2021. I was getting ready to move to France with my wife. After I moved to France, I spent my time studying French (it's a hard, hard language...). Well, until September 2021. When I could trust my level of French, I started to look for a job; as a teacher or a waiter (and all the professions in between)... I have been rejected or have not gotten a call from countless of job offers. So I decided. Decided to follow my dreams and study again... In October 2021, I started my formation to become a web developer.

My Experience

My first working experience was a sellerman at a Vodafone shop in Mersin.
I worked there in the first 2 years of my studies.
In the 3rd year of my studies, I went to Valencia, Spain to do an internship via Erasmus in June 2019.
I was an English teacher for kids, teenagers and adults for 3 months. Might be the best 3 months of my life...
Right after I came back to Turkey in September 2019, I started working in 2 different kid's academies as part-time English teacher until February 2020.
In the meantime, I was in my 4th year at university.
After I graduated from the university, I started to work in a language academy as full-time teacher from February 2020 to September 2020.
But I knew that, this was not my dream job.
